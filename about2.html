OUR HISTORY

In 1969, SAPPHIRE, a small spinning mill was established. Over the next half century, the business grew and gained worldwide recognition for producing premium-quality fabric, crafted with dedication and excellence. Despite the challenges faced by the textile industry, SAPPHIRE remained steadfast in its pursuit of innovation and continued to thrive. In 2014, SAPPHIRE the brand was born after fifty years of experience in the industry and a steadfast devotion to quality. The launch of SAPPHIRE marked a significant milestone for the company and a new chapter in the brand's journey. The company's focus shifted to producing clothing that was both stylish and functional while maintaining its premium-quality standards. Today, SAPPHIRE is a globally recognized brand known for its commitment to quality and its passion for creating exceptional clothing. Despite its success, the company remains true to its roots and continues to prioritize excellence in all aspects of its operations. While the journey may have started in a small spinning mill, SAPPHIRE's story continues to unfold, and the future holds endless possibilities for this remarkable brand.

HALAL RESPONSIBILITY

We are committed to bringing the best to our clients. Each item in the Masarrat Makeup collection is manufactured with the best quality Halal ingredients, making it the first ever Halal Certified Makeup Brand in Pakistan. Halal is an Arabic word meaning "Permissible" or "Lawful". We have refrained from using any haram animal substances or intoxicants in our products. We are committed to upholding our halal standards and integrity throughout the manufacturing and production process which includes checking for cross contamination, points of contact, transportation etc.
